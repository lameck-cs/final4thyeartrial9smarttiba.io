About Smart Tiba Kenya

Smart Tiba Kenya is an AI-powered Ethnobotanical platform designed to preserve indigenous knowledge and empower local farmers. By using your smartphone camera, you can snap a photo of any wild herb or plant to instantly receive:

  • Accurate ethnobotanical identification in local Kenyan languages (Kalenjin, Luo, Kisii, etc.).
  • Scientific validation of traditional medicinal cures and properties.
  • Critical safety and toxicity warnings.
  • Cultivation and commercialization advice to turn "weeds" into wealth.
